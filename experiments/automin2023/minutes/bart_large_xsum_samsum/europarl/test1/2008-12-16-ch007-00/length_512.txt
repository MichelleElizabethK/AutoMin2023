ATTENDEES : PERSON275


SUMMARY
- The Secretary of State for European Affairs, Minister Le Maire, Commissioner Figel' and the Council are going to sign the legal acts relating to the Erasmus Mundus action programme for the enhancement of quality in higher education and the promotion of intercultural understanding through cooperation with third countries.
- The European Union has increased funding for the Erasmus Mundus programme by nearly EUR 950 million for five years.
- The scope of the programme will be extended to include doctoral programmes and European students will receive more financial support.
