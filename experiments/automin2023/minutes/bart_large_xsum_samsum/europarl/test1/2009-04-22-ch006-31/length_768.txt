SUMMARY
- Amendment 39 to Directive 2006/42/EC on machinery sets out the requirements with which the machinery for application should comply in order to limit the adverse effects of pesticides on the environment, the operator and the surroundings.
- CEN will develop harmonised standards for each category of machinery for pesticide application
