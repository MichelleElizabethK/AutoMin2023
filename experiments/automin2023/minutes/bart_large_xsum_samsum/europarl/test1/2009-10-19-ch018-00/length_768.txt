SUMMARY
- The next item is the joint debate on: the report by Mr Busuttil on the proposal for a Council decision on the conclusion of the Agreement between the European Community and the Republic of Mauritius on the short-stay visa waiver.
- There is an agreement between the European Union and its citizens and the citizens of six different countries for a visa waiver.
- It is valid for a maximum stay of three months within a six-month period.
- It applies to European Union citizens who travel to these countries and vice-versa to The six non-EU countries - Antigua and Barbuda, the Bahamas, Barbados, Mauritius, Saint Kitts and Nevis and the Seychelles - were transferred from the negative list to the positive list of Regulation (EC) No 539/2001.
- The Confederal Group of the European United Left - Nordic Green Left is in favour of European Union agreements with these six countries and, reciprocally, EU citizens will be exempted from the short-stay visa requirement.
- The GUE/NGL Group also supports Simon Busuttil's report The joint debate is closed.
- The vote will take place tomorrow.
