DATE : 2023-05-01
ATTENDEES : PERSON068, PERSON069


SUMMARY
- PERSON068 and the shadow rapporteurs visited 26 detention centres in 10 EU Member States and noted several key points.
- They called for respect for clear, fair, effective and proportionate asylum procedures, and they called for the implementation of a permanent system of visits and inspections of detention
