DATE : 2023-05-01
ATTENDEES : PERSON006


SUMMARY
- The European Parliament congratulates the Colombian army on the release of Ingrid Betancourt and other hostages who were held hostage by FARC.
- Ingrid Betancourt was one of the finalists for the Sakharov Prize for Freedom of Thought in 2006.
- She was kidnapped in Colombia.
- Jean-Pierre Jouyet, the French Minister of State for European Affairs and representative of the new Presidency, is in the European Parliament.
