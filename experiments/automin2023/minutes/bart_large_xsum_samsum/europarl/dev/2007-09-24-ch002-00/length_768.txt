SUMMARY
- Lord Bethell died on 8 September at the age of 69, following a long and courageous battle with Parkinson's disease.
- Lord Bethell was a member of the European Parliament from 1975 to 1994 and again from 1999 to 2003.
- He was a Vice-President of the Political Affairs Committee.
