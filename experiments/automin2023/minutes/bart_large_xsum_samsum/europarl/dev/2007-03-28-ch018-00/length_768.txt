SUMMARY
- The next item is the report by Mr Itälä on the Committee on Budgets on the guidelines for the 2008 budget procedure.
- The main idea behind the budget for 2008 is to ensure that 2008 is the year of the taxpayers.
- The level of inflation needs to be raised to 2007 levels.
- There are many commitments regarding buildings and information policy.
- The local media should be able to visit Parliament more often.
- The proposal to reduce the consumption of paper and energy resources at the European Parliament and increase air-conditioning in the summer is supported by the group of Christian Democrats and European Democrats.
- The proposal also proposes to make electronic versions of many documents available on request instead of being automatically distributed to all Next year's budgeting level for the European Parliament will be the same level as in 2007.
- It will probably be the last year in which the surplus of up to 20% in the EU's administrative budget can be used for the purchase of buildings.
- The debate is closed.
- The vote will take place tomorrow at 11.00 a.m.
