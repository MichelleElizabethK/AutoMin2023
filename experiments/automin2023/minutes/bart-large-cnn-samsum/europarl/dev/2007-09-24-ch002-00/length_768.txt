ATTENDEES : PERSON006


SUMMARY
- Nicholas Bethell was a Member of the European Parliament from 1975 to 1994 and again from 1999 to 2003.
- He died on 8 September at the age of 69 after a long and courageous battle against Parkinson's disease.
- He devoted himself energetically to many issues, on which he also drafted reports.
- He visited the Soviet Union and made friends with leading dissidents, such as Alexander Solzhenitsyn, Andrei Sakharov, Anatoly Shcharansky and Alexander Ginsburg.
