SUMMARY
- The debate on the future of the common agricultural policy was supposed to be held today, but it has been postponed until November.
- It concerns all the countries in the European Union, but none more than the United Kingdom.
- It is unfair to conclude the debate and vote on the 20% voluntary modulation of direct payments to farmers with some form of contingency vote.
- PERSON039, on behalf of his group, supports the motion to postpone the debate and the vote until November.
- PERSON042, as rapporteur on financial planning, would like to endorse the motion as well as what Mr Swoboda has said.
- PERSON043, as President of this sitting, proposes to withdraw the joint debate on the reports by Mr Mulder and Mr Goepel.
