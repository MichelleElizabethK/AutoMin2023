SUMMARY
- Loyola de Palacio, Vice-President of the European Commission, Minister in the Spanish Government and head of the People's Party's list in the 1999 elections, has died.
- Loyola was a forthright person, a determined and strong negotiator, who was always true to her values and the values of Europe.
- Mrs de Palacio died suddenly.
- She was vice-president of the European Commission between 1999 and 2004.
- She worked to promote European transport and European energy.
- She established fruitful cooperation with the European Parliament and with all the MEPs.
- She also worked with President Barroso to complete the trans-European networks.
