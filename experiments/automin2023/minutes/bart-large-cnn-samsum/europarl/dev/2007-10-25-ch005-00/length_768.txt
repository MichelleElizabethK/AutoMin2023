DATE : 2023-05-14
ATTENDEES : PERSON045


SUMMARY
- The Conference of Presidents unanimously chose Salih Mahmoud Osman as the winner of the Sakharov Prize.
- Mr Osman is a human-rights lawyer and member of the Sudanese Organisation against Torture.
- He has been defending Darfur's victims for more than 20 years.
- The formal presentation of the prize will take place in Strasbourg on Tuesday, 11 December.
- Anna Politkovskaya will be honoured as a tribute.
