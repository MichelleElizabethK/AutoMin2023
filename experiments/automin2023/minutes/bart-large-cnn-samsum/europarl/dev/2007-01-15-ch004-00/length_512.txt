DATE : 2023-05-14
ATTENDEES : PERSON009


SUMMARY
- ETA has broken the ceasefire it had declared last March.
- ETA killed Carlos Alonso Palate and Diego Armando Estacio, two Ecuadoreans who had found their place in Spanish society and supported their families in Ecuador.
- The European Parliament expresses its sympathy and condolences to the families and friends of the two men who were murdered.
