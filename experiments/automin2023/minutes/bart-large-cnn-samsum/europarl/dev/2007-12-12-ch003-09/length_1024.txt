DATE : 2023-05-14
ATTENDEES : PERSON072


SUMMARY
- The Commission will give priority to the necessary procedure to allow for a rapid decision on claims referring to children's development and health.
- The European Food Safety Authority will also be given priority to processing the evaluation of such claims.
- The Commission confirms that, pending the establishment of nutrient profiles, the process of evaluating such claims can start without delay.
