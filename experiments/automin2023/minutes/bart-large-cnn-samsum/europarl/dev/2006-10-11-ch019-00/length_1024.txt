SUMMARY
- The report on the protection and welfare of animals in the period 2006-2010 was adopted unanimously by the Committee on Agriculture and Rural Development on 12 September.
- The objective of the Action Plan on the Protection and Welfare of Animals is to bring together Community activities spread across different Commission services, councils and policy areas and structure them in accordance with uniform guidelines.
- The report supports endeavours to replace animal testing with alternative methods on the basis of further research.
