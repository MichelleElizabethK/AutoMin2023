DATE : 2023-05-14
ATTENDEES : PERSON014, PERSON015, PERSON016, PERSON017


SUMMARY
- PERSON014 informs the President of the creation of a new political group in the European Parliament, 'Identity, Traditions and Sovereignty'.
- This group is composed of twenty Members from more than a fifth of the Member States.
- The composition of this group will be published in the Minutes of this part-session.
- The interview given by Mrs Mussolini to APA news agency on 8 January contradicts Rule 29 of the Rules of Procedure.
- The Identity, Tradition and Sovereignty Group has been set up.
- The declaration is in favour of maintaining European identity and the identities of our countries.
- It also includes non-confessional reference to Christian values and human rights.
- The Declaration will be published in the Official Journal and signed by the members of the group.
