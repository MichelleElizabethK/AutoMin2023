DATE : 2023-05-14
ATTENDEES : PERSON146


SUMMARY
- The Committee on Economic and Monetary Affairs wants the vote on adopting IFRS 8 as a replacement for the IAS 14 standard to be postponed until the second part-session in September.
- The Commission services have agreed to review their timetable and have consented to an impact study being carried out.
- The Committee would like the Commission to resubmit the implementing measure with the results of the impact study on 10 September.
