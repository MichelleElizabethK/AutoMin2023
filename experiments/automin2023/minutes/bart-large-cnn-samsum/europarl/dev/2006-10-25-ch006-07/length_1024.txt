SUMMARY
- The SIS II system is a central tool for law enforcement authorities and makes it possible to expand the Schengen area and abolish controls at internal borders.
- The Committee on Civil Liberties, Justice and Home Affairs voted on its position on 5 October, but did not approve the addition concerning authorities' access to the system.
- The Presidency has prepared a compromise that it hopes will be acceptable to all parties at first reading.
