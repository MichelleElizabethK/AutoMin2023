SUMMARY
- The European Commission postpones the implementation of the Directive on medical equipment for four years.
- Studies are currently in progress to determine how this equipment can be made compatible with the Directive.
- The Commission was reacting to new scientific evidence.
- The workers' representatives were involved in the discussions that took place with the social partners before the adoption of the Commission proposal.
