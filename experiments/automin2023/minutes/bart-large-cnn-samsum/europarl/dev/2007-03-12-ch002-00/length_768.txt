DATE : 2023-05-14
ATTENDEES : PERSON006, PERSON007


SUMMARY
- The ceremony commemorating the victims of terrorism was to be held here this afternoon before the plenary sitting began.
- The chairman of his group had no advance notice of this event.
- Neither the deputy chairman of the Committee on Civil Liberties, Justice and Home Affairs nor his group's coordinator had any word whatever of the event.
- He would have been happy to be present as a group chairman.
- PERSON007, President of this House, is dissatisfied with the way the procedure was carried out.
- He wants to compare the procedure with what was done last year and this year.
- The Chamber was packed with 580 young people.
- Next year, he wants to try for a bit more transparency.
