DATE : 2023-05-14
ATTENDEES : PERSON089


SUMMARY
- A Mauritanian delegation, led by Mr Ould Tolba, is currently visiting the European Parliament.
- The visit is the first in Europe for almost 10 years.
- The European Union has been paying close attention to the recent political transition in Mauritania.
- The Parliament congratulates Mauritania on the democratic election of its Head of State and Parliament.
