SUMMARY
- Parliament will vote on the joint proposals from the legal services of Parliament, the Commission and the Council.
- It would be more logical to adopt a directive on tractors enshrining general requirements and safeguards.
- The details could then be specified in standards.
- The principle of adopting general directives with reference to European standards has already been applied in more than 25 other sectors.
