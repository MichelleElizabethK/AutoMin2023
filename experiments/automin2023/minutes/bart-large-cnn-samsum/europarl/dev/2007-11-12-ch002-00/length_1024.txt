ATTENDEES : PERSON006


SUMMARY
- Eight people were killed by anti-personnel mines in Finland.
- It's the 10th anniversary of the signing of the Ottawa Convention on the Prohibition of the Use, Stockpiling, Production and Transfer of Anti-Personnel Mines and on their Destruction.
- 122 states signed the treaty on 3 and 4 December 1997 in Ottawa.
- Europe has made an important contribution to implementation of the Convention.
- The European Union invested more than a billion euros in the battle against landmines.
