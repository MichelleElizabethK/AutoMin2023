DATE : 2023-05-14
ATTENDEES : PERSON006


SUMMARY
- Eight people were killed by anti-personnel mines in Finland.
- The 10th anniversary of the signing of the Ottawa Convention on the Prohibition of the Use, Stockpiling, Production and Transfer of Anti-Personnel Mines and on their Destruction is coming up in a few weeks.
- 122 states signed the treaty on 3 and 4 December 1997.
- The International Campaign to Ban Landmines was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 1997.
- The international campaign against landmines has been very successful.
- Europe has made an important contribution to implementation of the Ottawa Convention.
- The European Union invested more than a billion euros in the battle against the use of anti-personnel mines from 1997 to 2004.
- The Council of the European Union is about to adopt a new joint action on the issue.
