SUMMARY
- Today is the International Day against the Death Penalty.
- The Council of Europe, with its 47 Member States, and with the support of the European Parliament and the European Commission, has declared 10 October the European Day.
- The European Parliament welcomes the nurses from Bulgaria and the doctors who escaped the death penalty in Libya a short time ago.
- They were falsely accused of having deliberately infected hundreds of Libyan children with HIV.
- The EU Parliament has held many debates on this issue and adopted three resolutions this year.
- The Olympic Games in Beijing will be an opportunity to promote the abolition of the death sentence.
