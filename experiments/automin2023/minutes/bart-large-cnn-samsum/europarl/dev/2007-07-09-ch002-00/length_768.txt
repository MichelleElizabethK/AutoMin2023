SUMMARY
- On 29 and 30 June there were renewed terrorist attacks in London and Glasgow.
- On 2 July a suicide bombing claimed further victims in Yemen.
- The European Parliament will discuss Jeanine Hennis-Plasschaert's report on the draft directive that will require Member States to identify important infrastructures in such areas as energy, health, communications and transport and protect them against terrorist attacks.
