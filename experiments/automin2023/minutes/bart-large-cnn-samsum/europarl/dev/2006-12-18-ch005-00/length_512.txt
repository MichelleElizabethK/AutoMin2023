DATE : 2023-05-14
ATTENDEES : PERSON015, PERSON016


SUMMARY
- The Prime Minister of Finland and the President of the Council of Europe will sign the REACH programme and the Seventh Framework Research Programme in Parliament.
- The signing is the final stage in the Union's complicated legislative process before the acts are published in the Official Journal.
- Mr Vanhanen will say a few words.
- The REACH Regulation will mean a complete revision of EU legislation on chemicals.
- European Chemicals Agency will be created to deal with such tasks as registration of substances.
- The European Parliament's cooperation in the negotiations on the framework research programme will get under way on schedule from the start of 2007.
