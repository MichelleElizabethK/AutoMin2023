DATE : 2023-05-14
ATTENDEES : PERSON053


SUMMARY
- The Council accepted the essential content of the Regulation on the funding of parties and foundations at European level last Friday.
- This morning, a trialogue took place with the Commission and the Council and they reached agreement on the questions that remained to be settled.
- The vote on the Regulation will be postponed until 29 November at the next plenary in Brussels.
