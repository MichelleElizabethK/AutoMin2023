SUMMARY
- The report on the action programme will enter into effect on 1 January of next year.
- It will focus on new Member States that do not have as long a tradition in the area of consumer protection.
- It also focuses on the older consumer and on the vulnerable consumer.
- It reproduces the agreement of the House and the institutions.
