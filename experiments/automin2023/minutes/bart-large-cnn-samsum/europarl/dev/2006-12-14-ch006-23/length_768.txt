SUMMARY
- The establishment of a Return Fund is conditional on the need to adopt a returns directive by the end of next year.
- If the Directive is not adopted, Parliament may make use of its budgetary entitlements and suspend payments to the Return Fund in the 2008 budget.
- The European Commission is open to discussions on improvements to the proposal.
