DATE : 2023-05-14
ATTENDEES : PERSON106, PERSON107, PERSON108


SUMMARY
- PERSON106 urges the House to vote in favour of the Return Fund.
- The Council will work with the European Parliament to reach a consensus on a Directive on common standards and procedures in Member States for returning illegally remaining third country nationals.
- It is one of the priorities of the Council and the Commission.
- The European Commission is open to discussions on improvements to the European Parliament and Council directive on common standards and procedures in Member States for returning illegally staying third country nationals.
- The Commission will propose to include the implementation of legislation in the field of return as one of the priorities for the strategic guidelines for the intervention of the Return Fund in the period 2008-13.
