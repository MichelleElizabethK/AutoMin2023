SUMMARY
- Rule 45(2) was recently amended to prohibit amendments to motions for resolutions contained in own-initiative reports.
- If a group wishes to amend one line or one paragraph, it has to table a separate alternative motion for a resolution.
- The Mikko report on concentration and pluralism in the media is the first report approved under this procedure.
- PERSON101 thanks everybody who contributed to the report on media pluralism and concentration in the European Union.
- PERSON102 wants to express the People's Party's opinion in the Parliamentary debate, but she was not given the floor.
