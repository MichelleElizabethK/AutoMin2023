SUMMARY
- The Directive 2006/42/EC on machinery for pesticide application is to limit the adverse effects of pesticides on the environment, the operator and the surroundings.
- It will be contained in standards developed by the European Committee for Standardization at the request of the Commission.
- Amendment 39 has been adopted.
- The Commission welcomes the compromise Amendment 39.
- CEN will develop harmonised standards for each category of machinery for pesticide application based on the best available techniques for preventing unintended exposure of the environment to pesticides.
- The standards will provide technical specifications for the fitting of mechanical shielding, tunnel spraying and air-assistance systems for spraying.
