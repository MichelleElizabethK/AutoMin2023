SUMMARY
- The European Parliament will vote on an amendment to the calendar of part-sessions for 2009 regarding the week before Orthodox Easter.
- The proposal is not admissible as there is no proposal for the European Parliament to meet in this particular week.
- However, it does not imply a preliminary decision concerning the proposed part-session from 20 to 23 April.
- PERSON121 proposes to proceed as Mr Daul and Mr Swoboda have proposed.
- The constituent sitting of Parliament will take place on 14 July 2009.
- Members from some countries have concerns that they will not achieve the requisite five years for pension rights if they work on July 14th.
