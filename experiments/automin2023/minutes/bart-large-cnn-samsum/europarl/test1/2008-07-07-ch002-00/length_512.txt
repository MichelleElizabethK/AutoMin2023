DATE : 2023-05-02
ATTENDEES : PERSON006


SUMMARY
- Ingrid Betancourt and other hostages were released on 2 July 2008 thanks to a military operation by the Colombian army.
- The European Parliament is delighted for them and their families.
- The release means the end of a harrowing time and is a reminder that we must never give up hope.
- Ingrid Betancourt was one of the three finalists for the Sakharov Prize for Freedom of Thought in 2006.
- She was invited to visit the European Parliament.
- Jean-Pierre Jouyet is the French Minister of State for European Affairs and representative of the new Presidency.
