DATE : 2023-05-02
ATTENDEES : PERSON149, PERSON150, PERSON151


SUMMARY
- Cecilia Malmström, the Minister for European Affairs, will sign a legislative package on behalf of the Council.
- The package includes a capital requirements directive, a regulation on credit rating agencies, a new regulation on cross-border payments and a decision establishing a new programme to support specific activities in the field of financial services, financial reporting and audit.
- PERSON150 and Minister Malmström are going to sign a number of legal acts.
- The acts are central to the EU's response to the financial and economic crisis that has hit Europe hard.
- The Czech Presidency worked very hard to get these acts in place within a short time.
