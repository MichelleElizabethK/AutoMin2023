DATE : 2023-05-02
ATTENDEES : PERSON072, PERSON073


SUMMARY
- PERSON072 withdraws his name from the Socialist Group's alternative motion for a resolution on the Auken report.
- He will vote in favour of the report instead.
- He has been working on the issue for five years and will vote against both alternative motions for resolution and for the report.
- The report on the impact of urbanisation on the environment and property rights of EU citizens living in Spain has been approved by the Committee on Petitions by a two-thirds majority.
- The report deserves a proper debate in which all opinions can be heard.
- The European Parliament has already committed itself to respecting the rights of European citizens.
