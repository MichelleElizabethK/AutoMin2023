DATE : 2023-05-02
ATTENDEES : PERSON275


SUMMARY
- PERSON275 is going to sign the legal acts relating to the Erasmus Mundus action programme for the enhancement of quality in higher education and the promotion of intercultural understanding through cooperation with third countries.
- Minister Le Maire thanks Commissioner Figel' for pushing the programme forward.
- The European Union is now in a position to meet the growing demand for the Erasmus Mundus programme, thanks to increased funding of nearly EUR 950 million for five years.
- The scope of the programme will be extended to include doctoral programmes and European students will get even more financial support.
