DATE : 2023-05-02

SUMMARY
- PERSON497 asks the President to end the debate on the oral question by Mr Ouzký, on behalf of the Committee on the Environment, Public Health and Food Safety, to the Commission: Commission's strategy for the third meeting of the Parties to the Convention on Access to Information, Public Participation in Decision-Making and Access to Justice in Environmental Matters (B6-0157/2008).
- The rapporteur for the Aarhus Convention is not present.
- Mr Ouzký is not presenting the question.
- The Commissioner and two main speakers are present, including two distinguished Members of the European Parliament from the PPE-DE and the PSE Groups.
- PERSON504 suggests that the debate should be stopped and that the person who answers the question without the substance of the question can continue.
- The third Meeting of the Parties to the Aarhus Convention will be held in Riga in June.
- The European Community is a Party to the Convention since May 2005.
- The Commission's priorities for the third Meeting are to adopt a Long-term Strategic Plan and appoint high quality members to the Compliance Committee.
