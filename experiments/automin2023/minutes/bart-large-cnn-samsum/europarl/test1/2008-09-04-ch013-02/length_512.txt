DATE : 2023-05-02
ATTENDEES : PERSON253, PERSON257, PERSON258, PERSON259, PERSON260, PERSON261, PERSON262


SUMMARY
- PERSON253 proposes two amendments to the resolution on the UN moratorium on the death penalty.
- He asks for the words 'starting to notably work together with the UNHCR and others' to be deleted from the resolution and for a Special Envoy of the Secretary-General to be appointed to monitor the situation.
- PERSON258 calls on the House not to vote for an amendment that would delete the mention of the Geneva Convention for prisoners in Camp Ashraf.
- PERSON259 and UEN Group are also opposed to the amendment.
- UEN has also tabled an oral amendment to Article 9.
