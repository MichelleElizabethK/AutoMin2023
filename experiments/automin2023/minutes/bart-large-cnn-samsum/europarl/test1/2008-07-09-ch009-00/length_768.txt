DATE : 2023-05-02
ATTENDEES : PERSON253, PERSON254, PERSON255


SUMMARY
- The European Parliament is signing a legal instrument on the European satellite navigation programmes EGNOS and Galileo.
- Galileo is to comprise a network of 30 satellites and a ground-control infrastructure.
- European institutions found solutions that made it possible to establish such a complex technical system.
- EU institutions agreed on Community funding of EUR 3.4 billion for Galileo.
- PERSON254 congratulates the Slovenian Presidency on the adoption of the Regulation on Galileo.
- The Regulation will enable the deployment of the publicly funded global satellite positioning system.
- The management of the Galileo programme will be placed under the political control of Parliament and the Council.
- The French Presidency is determined to work very closely with the European Parliament.
