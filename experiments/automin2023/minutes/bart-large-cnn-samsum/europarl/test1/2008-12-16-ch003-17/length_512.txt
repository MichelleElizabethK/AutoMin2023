DATE : 2023-05-02
ATTENDEES : PERSON029


SUMMARY
- The Commission welcomes the agreement reached in first reading on the alignment of existing legislative acts in the new comitology procedure with scrutiny.
- The time limits within which the European Parliament and the Council may oppose a draft measure have been curtailed to four weeks or one month.
- The Commission commits itself to take into account the need for a plenary session before the expiration of the curtailed time limits.
- The Commission regrets the decision to apply the regulatory procedure with scrutiny to Article 8(1) of Regulation (EC) No 2111/2005.
- The Commission stresses that the implementing measures referred to in that article are procedural and administrative in nature and provide details of rules already laid down in the basic act.
