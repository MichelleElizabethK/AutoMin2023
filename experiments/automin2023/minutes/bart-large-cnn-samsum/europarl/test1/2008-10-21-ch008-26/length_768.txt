SUMMARY
- PERSON156 proposes an amendment limiting the right of the spouses to choose the law applicable to divorce and legal separation.
- PPE-DE Group wanted to limit the choice of law so as to be in conformity with the fundamental rights defined in the Treaties and in the Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union and the principle of public policy.
- The agreement between the Christian Democrats and European Democrats, the Socialist Group in the European Parliament, the Group of the Greens/ European Free Alliance and the Alliance of Liberals and Democrats for Europe was that if they accept the oral amendment, the other P PE-DE amendments will be withdrawn.
- Amendments lapsed.
- Once they have lapsed, Madam Muscardini cannot put them to the vote, because they are no longer valid.
- - PERSON166 says that the amendments had lapsed and cannot be put to a vote.
