SUMMARY
- The European Parliament's Committee on Legal Affairs has adopted the new recital 12a.
- It states that the prior grant of an authorisation, licence or concession should not constitute a defence in such circumstances.
- The Council fully understands the concerns expressed in the amendment and is confident that Member States will take due account of these concerns.
- The declarations form part of the compromise which we have agreed on the legislative procedure.
- The text will be approved by the Council if it is adopted today.
- The Chair of Coreper confirms that this text will also be approved if adopted.
- This will give us the legislative success we want to achieve.
