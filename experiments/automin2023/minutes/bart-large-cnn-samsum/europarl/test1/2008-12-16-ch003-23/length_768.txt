- PERSON041, rapporteur, wants to table three oral amendments to the European Parliament's law regarding parliamentary assistants.
- Commission confirms agreement on the text for a statute for parliamentary assistants and promises to defend it until its final approval by the Council.
- Parliament will now proceed to a roll-call vote to close an issue that has been pending for almost 30 years.
- The transition to new conditions of employment of accredited parliamentary assistants does not require an increase in the appropriations entered in the European Parliament section of the general budget of the European Union, subject to indexation.
- The Committee on Legal Affairs has found the right balance between the free and fundamental choice of Members to choose their own assistants and transparency.
