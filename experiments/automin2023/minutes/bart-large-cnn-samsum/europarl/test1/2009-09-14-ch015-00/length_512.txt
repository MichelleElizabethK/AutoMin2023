DATE : 2023-05-02
ATTENDEES : PERSON020, PERSON021, PERSON022, PERSON023, PERSON024, PERSON025, PERSON026


SUMMARY
- The final draft agenda for the Conference of Presidents has been distributed.
- A number of non-attached Members want to meet with the President to elect their delegates to the Conference.
- The President will contact them during the sitting to make the necessary arrangements.
- The meeting will take place on Wednesday.
- The Conference of Presidents voted to place the election of the President of the Commission on Wednesday's agenda.
- On Tuesday afternoon they will hold a debate on the President's proposals.
- The referendum in Ireland is due to be held in 22 days' time.
- 62% of Irish people are ready to vote in favour of the Treaty of Lisbon.
- The Polish President has declared that he will sign the Treaty after the Irish referendum.
- The Czech Constitutional Court declared that it will speed up decision making on the complaints against the Treaty.
- PERSON024, Mr Cohn-Bendit and Mr Daul discuss a motion put forward by their group in the Conference of Presidents last week.
- The Council wants to elect the President on the basis of the Treaty of Nice and apply the Lisbon Treaty to Commissioners, which would mean a President of the Commission elected on a different legal basis than his Commissioners.
- The vote should be delayed until the Council agrees what it wants.
- PERSON026 asks for the election of the European Parliament to be postponed until the Treaty of Lisbon comes into force.
- He also wants to put in place a Commission on the basis of the Nice Treaty.
- He wants to vote on Wednesday for the President of the Commission.
