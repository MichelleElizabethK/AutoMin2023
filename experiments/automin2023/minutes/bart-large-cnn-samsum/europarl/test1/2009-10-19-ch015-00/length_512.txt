SUMMARY
- The final version of the draft agenda for the Conference of Presidents' meeting of 15 October 2009 has been distributed.

- There will be a vote without debate on a report by Mrs Wallis concerning the immunity of Mr Siwiec.
- The report by Mr Speroni was adopted by a large majority and the complaint was filed with the Court of Justice of the European Communities.
- There is no discussion on the agenda because of the absence of any kind of motion on this matter.
- The European Parliament wants to change the title of the resolution on freedom of expression in Europe.
- The issue on Italy within the resolution is essentially a national political debate.
- Christian Democrats in the European People's Party (Christian Democrats) have confidence in their colleagues in the parliament in Rome and trust in the Roman courts.
- On Thursday, the Council will discuss the terrible death penalties in Iran and the attack that cost 42 people their lives.
- The Council is against violence, against the death penalty and against terrorist violence as a matter of principle.
- Mr President should condemn terrorist violence in general even if directed against the government bodies of Iran.
