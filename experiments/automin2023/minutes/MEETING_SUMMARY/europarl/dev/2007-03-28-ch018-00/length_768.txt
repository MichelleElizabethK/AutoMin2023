SUMMARY
- The next item is the report by Mr Itälä on behalf of the Committee on Budgets on the guidelines for the 2008 budget procedure.
- The main idea behind the budget for 2008 is to ensure that 2008 is the year of the taxpayers.
- The level of inflation needs to be raised to 2007 levels.
- There are many commitments regarding the buildings and the information policy.
- The local media should be invited to Parliament.
- Translation services are also important.
- The European Parliament proposes to reduce the use of paper and energy resources and increase air-conditioning in order to save money.
- It also proposes to introduce electronic versions of many documents to be made available on request.
- Next year's budgeting level for the European Parliament will remain in principle at the 2007 level.
- It will be the last year in which the surpluses from the EU's administrative budget can be used for the purchase of buildings.
- The European Parliament will vote on the report on the financial resources of the European institutions tomorrow at 11.00 a.m.
