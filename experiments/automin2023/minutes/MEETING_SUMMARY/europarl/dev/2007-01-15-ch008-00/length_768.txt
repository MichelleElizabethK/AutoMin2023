SUMMARY
- The new political group "Identity, Traditions and Sovereignty" in the European Parliament is composed of twenty-five MEPs from more than a fifth of the Member States.
- The group's objectives are to preserve European identity, traditions and sovereignty, financial transparency and human rights.
- It Parliament does not evaluate the political affinity of members of a group.
- The group has made a declaration of principles and political statement.
- It will be published in the Official Journal and signed by all the members.
