DATE : 2023-05-01
ATTENDEES : PERSON008


SUMMARY-
 -The European Union is committed to taking appropriate measures in conformity with the Cotonou Agreement, including the suspension of the agreement with Turkmenistan, if necessary.
  The European Commission is aware of Parliament's concerns about the democracy and human rights situation in Turkmenistan.
  Both the Interim Trade Agreement and the Political Protection Agreement (PPA) contain human rights provisions.
  The Commission is going to monitor the situation closely.
  If Parliament recommends suspending the agreements, Parliament wants to withhold its support for the agreement with Turkmenistan.
  The President-in-Office of the Council refused to back Parliament's demands.
  The Commission and the Council would consider suspending or revoking the agreement if Parliament calls for it.
  The Group of the Greens/European
