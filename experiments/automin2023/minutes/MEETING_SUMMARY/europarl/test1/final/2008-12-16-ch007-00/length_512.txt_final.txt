- President Hollande thanks the French Presidency, the Commission, Committee on Culture and Education and rapporteur De Sarne   Minuted by: Team Synapse
- The scope of the programme will be extended to include doctoral programmes.
- The European Union has increased funding for the Erasmus Mundus programme by nearly EUR 950 million for five years.
