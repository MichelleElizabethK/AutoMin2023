DATE : 2023-05-01
ATTENDEES : PERSON006


SUMMARY
- The European Parliament is delighted for Ingrid Betancourt and other hostages who were released by Colombian army on Wednesday, 2 July 2008.
- Ingrid Betancourt was one of the finalists for the Sakharov Prize for Freedom of Thought in 2006.
- She was also an activist for peace in Colombia.
- The European Parliament invited her to visit the European Parliament.
- Jean-Pierre Jouyet, French Minister of State for European Affairs
