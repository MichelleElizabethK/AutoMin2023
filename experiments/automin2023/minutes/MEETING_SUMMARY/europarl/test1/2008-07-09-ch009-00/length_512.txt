DATE : 2023-05-01
ATTENDEES : PERSON253, PERSON254, PERSON255


SUMMARY
- The President of the European Parliament, Jean-Pierre Jouyet, is signing the legal instrument on the Galileo programme.
- The Regulation on Galileo has been adopted by the European Parliament.
- It will enable the deployment of the publicly funded global satellite positioning system.
- The management of the Galileo programme will be under the political control of Parliament and the Council.
- The French Presidency proposes to work closely with the European Commission and the The PERSON255 and Jouyet will sign the Galileo Regulation.
- They will ask Commissioner Ferrero-Waldner, Angelika Niebler, chairman of the Committee on Industry, Research and Energy, and Etelka Barsi-Pataky to join them.
